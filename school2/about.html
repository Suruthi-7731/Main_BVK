About Us

Nurturing Excellence Since 1995

  Vision :

     The portals of Bharathi Vidhyaa Kendra vividly radiate its vision to enable young minds to think, absorb, and respond to situations around them with equanimity,learning and growing with the ideals of a purpose-driven life.
The components of our vision include:
SHAKTHI: We uphold the belief in the transformative power of education as a transcending agent for our students, enhancing and reflecting the innate power within.
INVATION: We recognise that innovation is a prerequisite for survival in the modern era and ensure that our students are inculcated with the value of curiosity to address the challenges that emerge from time to time.
SIDDHI: We reinforce the quality of discipline and commitment for the young, to adopt necessary measures to achieve their aspirations with a positive mental a tude, based on the foundation of endurance and resilience, to work their words into significant achievements.

  Mission :

To create and prepare young individuals to become lifelong learners and responsible citizens ready to meet the challenges of the future by changing the world to suit their needs.

Guiding Principles:

  • Ensure our pupils are safe and happy
  • Expect respectful, kind and well-mannered conduct
  • Uphold the highest standards in all aspects
  • Provide ultimate learning environment and experiences
  • Accommodate and retain expert teaching professionals
  • Inculcate passion towards learning to pursue excellence
  • Influence the community with optimism
