Founder Vision & Mission

Founded on a powerful belief that education transforms lives, the Bhaarathi Group of schools began its journey from the humblest of beginnings. Established with a founding strength of just two students, the institution was nurtured with dedication, resilience and an unwavering sense of purpose.

Over 25 years of blossoming into two flourishing institutions under the Bhaarathi banner
Steadily growing in strength while remaining rooted in values of learning and character
Placing learning, character and excellence at the heart of education for every student

The vision continues to inspire every educator and administrator to provide quality education with moral values, ensuring that every child who walks through our doors becomes a responsible citizen equipped for life's challenges.
