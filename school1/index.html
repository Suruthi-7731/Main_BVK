Vision

The portals of Bharathi Vidhyaa Kendra vividly radiate its vision to enable young minds to think, absorb, and respond to situations around them with equanimity, learning and growing with the ideals of a purpose-driven life.
The components of our vision include:

SHAKTHI: We uphold the belief in the transformative power of education as a transcending agent for our students, enhancing and reflecting the innate power within.

INVATION: We recognise that innovation is a prerequisite for survival in the modern era and ensure that our students are inculcated with the value of curiosity to address the challenges that emerge from time to time.

SIDDHI: We reinforce the quality of discipline and commitment for the young, to adopt necessary measures to achieve their aspirations with a positve mental atitude, based on the foundation of endurance and resilience, to work their words into significant achievements.

Mission

BVK is dedicated to Provide a happy, caring, empowering environment, embedded with learner-centred education Excellence through inspirational teaching to enlighten all its pupils Exceptional professional standards to transform our students intellectually, physically, socially and morally as value-enshrined individuals reflecting self-esteem and self-confidence Smulating environment for the students to emerge as citizens of the world with care and compassion.

We Value...

Effort, creativity and endeavour directed towards the attainment of skill & knowledge. Initiative, ambition for excellence and progress.

Facilities

Our classrooms are spacious, well-ventilated, and designed to create a comfortable learning environment for students. Each classroom is equipped with modern teaching aids, smart boards, and audio-visual facilities to make lessons interactive and engaging.

Comfortable seating arrangements and proper lighting ensure that students can focus and participate actively. The classrooms are maintained with high standards of cleanliness and safety, providing a positive and disciplined atmosphere for effective learning.

World Class Library

The school has a well-equipped library to kindle the spirit of inquiry in our children.It has a wide collection of books,magazines and newspapers to cater to the needs of students.

Transport

Transport facilities are available for students on request.The charges are based on the distance from the school. This facility must be opted for the entire academic year.The pickup and drop points will be decided by the school authorities based on the number students in each area.

Team Sports

The sportive spirit of our students is kindled as exclusive practice and intensive coaching is given in football,kho-kho, volleyball and athletic events.Mastering a sports also gives learners confidence in above areas.
